BIOGRAPHY

Since 2015, Tom Coe has been a professional 3D generalist working on blockbuster films, feature animations, commercials, web content videos and animated televesion shows for clients such as Disney, Warner Bros., Paramount, Toyota, Lexus, Lincoln, Ford, and Audi. Currently located in the UK, Tom is employed as a Senior Environments TD at Framestore, working on How To Train Your Dragon. Prior to this, he was employed by production houses DNEG, MPC, Red Star 3D, Blink Industries, an earlier stint at Framestore, and Rotor Studios, working on projects such as (but not limited to) Here, No Time To Die, Wonder Woman 2, Detective Pikachu, The Lion King and a variety of TVCs.
